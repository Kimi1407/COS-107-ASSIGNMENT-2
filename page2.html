WHAT IS SOFTWARE ENGINEERING?

Software Engineering is a dynamic and in-demand course that equips students with the skills and knowledge to design, develop, and maintain complex software systems. Through a combination of theoretical foundations and practical applications, students learn about software methodologies, programming languages, data structure, algorithms and software testing. The course also emphasizes essential soft skills such as communication, team work and problem-solving. By studying software engineering, students can pursue exciting career opportunities in industries such as tech, healthcare, finance and many more, and play a key role in shaping the digital world of tomorrow.
